World, Why Won't You Say Hello Back?

by: Timmy Bohmert

I am a future web developer, and I am learning a lot of different features and capabilities that I never even knew existed. It is exciting, new, freshing, and enjoyable.

Though the different routes I have learned from taught various abilities, a common theme has been anoucing yours truly to the world. It has been enriching and delightful, freeing and open.

Yet, when will the world reply back? I have always known him as a stubborn one (he has refused to change shape for the past 4.5 billion years), but nothing. Not even a text or call to let me know he is safe?

Don't worry though, world, I still love you.
